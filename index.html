About Solvixy

Solvixy is an advanced AI-powered educational tool designed to help students solve problems across multiple subjects. Our mission is to make quality education accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or background.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Solvixy was created by a team of educators and technologists who recognized the need for personalized, accessible learning tools. We saw students struggling with complex problems and wanted to create a solution that could provide instant, step-by-step guidance.

Our Technology

Solvixy leverages cutting-edge AI technology to understand and solve problems across various subjects. Our system is continuously learning and improving to provide more accurate and helpful responses.
